Think like the herd!

Everyone answers the same question with one word. Match the most popular answer to score a point!

How to Play

1
A question is shown to all players — e.g., "Name a hot drink"
2
Each player takes the phone privately and types their one-word answer. Don't show others!
3
After everyone has answered, all answers are revealed at once.
4
The most popular answer wins — everyone who gave it scores a point!
5
If no answer is clearly the most popular (tie or all unique), nobody scores.
6
Answers are compared case-insensitively. Play as many rounds as you like!
